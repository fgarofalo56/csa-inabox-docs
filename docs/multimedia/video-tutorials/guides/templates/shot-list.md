## Technical Specifications

### Video Settings
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (4K for screen recordings)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps (60fps for animations)
- **Codec**: H.264
- **Bitrate**: 8-10 Mbps
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9

### Audio Settings
- **Sample Rate**: 48 kHz
- **Bit Depth**: 24-bit
- **Channels**: Stereo
- **Narration Level**: -18 dB
- **Music Level**: -30 dB
- **Format**: AAC

### Screen Recording Settings
- **Software**: OBS Studio / Camtasia
- **Cursor**: Highlight enabled
- **Hotkeys**: Configured
- **Browser**: Chrome (clean profile)
- **Zoom Level**: 100%
- **Resolution**: 4K downscaled to 1080p
