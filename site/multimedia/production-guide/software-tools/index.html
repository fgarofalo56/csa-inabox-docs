    Recording Software¶

    OBS Studio (Free, Open Source) - Screen recording and streaming - Scene composition - Multi-source capture - Plugin ecosystem

    Camtasia ($250) - Easy screen recording - Built-in editor - Annotations and callouts - Quiz and interactivity

    Video Editing¶

    DaVinci Resolve (Free/Paid) - Professional editing - Color grading - Fairlight audio - Fusion VFX

    Adobe Premiere Pro ($55/month) - Industry standard - Creative Cloud integration - Team collaboration - Extensive plugins

    Final Cut Pro ($300, Mac only) - Optimized for Mac - Magnetic timeline - Fast rendering

    Motion Graphics¶

    Adobe After Effects ($55/month) - Animation and VFX - Motion tracking - Compositing

    Blender (Free) - 3D animation - Motion graphics - Video editing

    • iZotope RX ($130-$1,000): Audio repair
